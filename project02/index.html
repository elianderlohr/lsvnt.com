Create a commercial publication that is so attractive that the reader will want to pick it up and bring it home with them.

I designed a magazine for a small organization working for environmental issues that as very little money but a great brand. « The Soup Factory » is a little shop where you can find soup to eat or to take away.
The content of this magazine includes various information
about each month, such as seasonal recipes or events
like exhibitions, concerts, markets, etc.
The logo represents
a soup’s bowl seen from above.
