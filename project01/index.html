How as a designer can I help citizens making ethical choices without denying their culture ?

I created ETIK, a Christmas program for supermarkets. The ETIK program is set up 3 weeks before Christmas so that the consumer adopts good practices before the festive season. their culture ?

The French are very attached to the tradition of the Christmas Eve meal. It is a moment of conviviality where we meet as a family to prepare a festive meal and to elaborate the best. However, this traditional meal has become a strong time of consumerism and the products are not always in accordance with our values. Etik is committed to reaching some of the Sustainable Development Goals (SDG).
