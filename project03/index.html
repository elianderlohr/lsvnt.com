DATE
March, 2019
TYPE
UX/UI Design


Developt a circular economy inside OVHcloud and propose an eco friendly cloud’s migration to their customers.

It is essential to give a second chance to the hardware, responsible for a large part of the energy expenses of digital. The major step in this project is the recovery of the customer’s used servers once the customer has migrated to the cloud.
OVHcloud calls on a partner who is part of a circular economy approach and helps the customer to dispose of its hardware in the most responsible way possible.
In order to present the project
to OVHcloud, I made a brandbook
which show all the observations,
findings and the new concept.
