PROJECT 04
DATE
April, 2021
TYPE
Packaging - Social Media


Being a graphic designer for Fitvia, I have developed all the visuals for the brand. As social media, websites, Amazon Brandstore and packaging.

I decided to play with an explosion of fruits which refer to the client’s experience when they drink their tea.
Fitvia 8 years birthday campaign.
The idea was to make the campaigns always dynamic
and lively while keeping the Fitvia graphic charter.
Sustainability is one of Fitvia’s goals. With the product team, we thought
about how the new packaging can be more sustainable.
We decided to go for 100% recyclable packaging and adapt the visuals
which already exist to this new product
I designed the new advent calendar. After creating the printing file,
I had to make this calendar alive with a fresh collage.
I keep Fitvia’s identity and created a design
that dives into the consumer in the world of Christmas.
